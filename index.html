Happy Valentine's Day

About Valentine's Day

The origin of the holiday

The holiday is named after Saint Valentine, who secretly married lovers in Rome.

First Valentine's card

The earliest known valentine was written in 1415 by Charles, Duke of Orleans, to his wife from prison in London.

Heart symbol

The image of a heart became a symbol of love in the Middle Ages, as it was believed that the heart itself was the center of feelings.

Red roses

These flowers are associated with Valentine's Day because, according to legend, they were the favorite flowers of the goddess of love, Venus.

Chocolate and love

Giving chocolate on Valentine's Day became popular thanks to the Cadbury company in the 19th century. Chocolate was believed to increase happiness.

Proposal Day

This is the day that most marriage proposals take place in the world. Many couples choose February 14th for their engagement.

Birds and love

In medieval Europe, it was believed that February 14th was the day when birds began to look for mates.

Holiday popularity

Valentine's Day is celebrated in over 50 countries around the world, although the traditions of the celebration can vary greatly.

Valentine's cards for friends and family

In Japan and South Korea, Valentine's Day is celebrated not only between lovers, but also among friends and colleagues.

Benefits of Valentine's Day

It reminds us of the importance of feelings – it is a great opportunity to confess your love, thank your loved ones and express your sincere emotions.
It creates an atmosphere of joy – on this day, streets, shops and homes are filled with warmth, pleasant surprises and a romantic mood.
Motivates to do good deeds – many people make surprises, give gifts and show more attention to those who are dear to them.
Adds variety to everyday life – even on a cold February Valentine's Day brings bright emotions, inspiration and a desire to create something special.
It's not just for lovers - you can celebrate this holiday with friends, family, or even just treat yourself to some nice little things.❤️
